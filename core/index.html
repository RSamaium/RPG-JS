Population Control

  • High Concept: Keep the kitten population under control by stopping them from reaching the outside of the box!
  • Player Experience Goals: Kitten management.
  • Genre: Arcade.
  • Game play: The player can move the trampolines back and fourth via the mouse to keep the kittens inside.
  • Prototype goal: Bouncing/multiplication of obstacles.
  • Features: It gets harder and harder as the game goes on.
  • Setting/Premise: It takes place in a box of kittens, where the kittens mature very quickly and begin to fall in love with each other and therefore reproduce quickly.
  • Story: No story
  • Target Audience: Kitten lovers.
  • Play time: A few minutes**. The game goes on indefinitely, players can reach the highest score they possible can by keeping the kittens in check.
  • Strategies: Move trampolines back and fourth rapidly.
  • Prototype Assets: Yes, except for the sounds and music. I got the music from The Pink Panther.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Breakout.
